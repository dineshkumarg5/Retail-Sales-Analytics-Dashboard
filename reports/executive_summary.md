✅ **Consistent Revenue Growth** — Business grew 21%+ YoY in 2017  
✅ **Technology Margins** — Phones, Copiers, Accessories delivering 17–37% margins  
✅ **West & East Regions** — Combined 60% of revenue with healthy margins

---

## ⚠️ 3 Things Needing Attention

🔴 **Furniture Losses** — Tables and Bookcases generating -$21K combined loss  
🔴 **Discount Abuse** — 30%+ discounts cause loss on 58% of those transactions  
🔴 **Central Region** — Lowest margin (7.9%) and significant discount usage

---

## 🎯 Top 3 Recommended Actions

| Priority | Action | Expected Impact |
|---|---|---|
| 🔴 1 | **Cap discounts at 20%** across all categories | +2–3% margin improvement |
| 🟡 2 | **Exit or reprice Tables sub-category** | +$17K annual profit recovery |
| 🟡 3 | **Launch RFM retention for At-Risk customers** | +8% revenue from existing base |
